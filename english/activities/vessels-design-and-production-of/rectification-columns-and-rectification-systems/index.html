Rectification columns and rectification systems

We are the producer of small laboratory or production rectification columns. These columns are design according the customer needs. We produce the whole rectification systems - the Boiler- the Column- the Cooler- the reflux control system. This whole completed can be equipped by control system based on process computer, or standard lab PC control system. We produce the column filled with different types of Rashlig rings ( easy maintenance and trouble free change of feedstock ). On request it is not problem to deliver column equipped with space- structurized filler.

RTUnits team produces atmospheric pressure rectification systems and the vacuum systems as well as.
