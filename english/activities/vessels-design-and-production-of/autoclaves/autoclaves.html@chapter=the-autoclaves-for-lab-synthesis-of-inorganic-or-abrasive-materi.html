Autoclaves

The wide area of our business is the design and production of autoclaves. Our autoclaves are design for different purposes.
All autoclaves are equipped by heating-cooling-mixing control unit. This unit is controlling the testing or production process. All is designed to provide easy and user friendly control of autoclave.

It is not problem to add automatically dataloging of temperature, pressure. The next possibility of control is to connected autoclave to process computer and has the "full control" of process.

The autoclaves for lab synthesis of inorganic or abrasive materials

For synthesis of inorganic crystalline materials ( zeolites and similar ) or abrasive materials we prepared specially shaking or mixing autoclaves equipped with Teflon insert. This type of autoclave is without sealing and it is designed for precision of temperature control. The volume of this type of reactor is from 100 ml to 1 litre. The control is similar like all our autoclaves.
