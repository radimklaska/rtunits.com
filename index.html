Our company is an expert in production, design and start-up of special research testing units. We have wide experience in the production of custom research units designed for special customer needs.

Our team provided the research equipment to chemical companies and universities and technical institutes.

The RTUnits team produced special testing rings for training of students, for synthesis of new materials, for testing of catalysts, for pilot study of new technologies and finally for production of high purities special chemical compounds in small quantities.

We are specialized in use high-end electronic control system, software and analytical support. We use the last science of progress to reach our aim. The team of our specialist can combine the experiences from different research areas. These conditions are excellent for building of unique research testing facilities.

The RTUnist is located in the European Union. We produce our products in EU quality with all necessary safety documentation. All parts of research testing units are according to EU standards and specifications.
